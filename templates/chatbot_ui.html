predict

AKOBot can currently only predict delays on Greater Anglia mainline trains from Norwich to London Liverpool Street on the Great Eastern Mainline. Unfortunately, if you enter a station not on the mainline, AKO will be unable to predict your delay.

On the train and need to ask ‘how long will I be delayed?’. Ask AKO to predict your delay based on National Rail data. (For trains from Norwich to London Liverpool Street on the Great Eastern Mainline only)
